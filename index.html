About Us

Welcome to Titans Badminton Klub

Titans Badminton Klub is an emerging sports organization dedicated to fostering a vibrant badminton community. Founded in late 2024, the club has quickly established itself as a recognized entity within the Swedish sports landscape.

Our Journey & Formation

The club began its journey on October 18, 2024. Since then, we have reached several key regulatory milestones to ensure a professional foundation for our members:

  • Federation Approval: Granted by Swedish Badminton Federation in January 2025.
  • Tax Registration: Fully approved as of January 27, 2025.

Competitive Play & Growth

We are committed to bringing our players to the competitive stage.

  • Tournament Integration: In August 2025, registered in official Tournament Software.
  • Active Participation: Players began competing in September 2025.

Current Programs

Titans Badminton Klub is actively expanding its reach:

  • Women’s Team: Weekday play group initiated with an initial core of three players.
  • Kids’ Training: Developing a structured training program for younger players.
